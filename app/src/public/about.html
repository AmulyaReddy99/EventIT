About EventIT

EventIT is an idea evolved out of personal interest to place all IT/ computer sciece relatede events under one roof. We have built this webappp in Hasura platform in 2017. With the help of Mr Tejas Reddy (BTech, IITJ) and the team Hasura for its provision of its tools, it is possible for us to buld this app in a very short span of time.

We begun our journey from June 2017. This app is built using node.js, Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, Postgresql and Hasura platform

On this site, you can go through the summary, get notifications and also Upload events
